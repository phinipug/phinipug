The goal

So what is this all about?

This is about experiencing EverQuest like you might experience an organized pickup game of Basketball or a round of golf. You know when to show up, where you need to be, and about how long you're going to be there.

Many of us have a limited time to play. Sometimes, just finding a group can take hours. On top of that, how many times have you arrived to the group (after a 20 minute run) only to have it tragically dissolve shortly after? Now repeat this process all over again...

Our 16 year old selves may have been able to suffer this until we found a decent group, but that was then and this is now.

Some people just tell me, "join a guild, dummy!" - While this helps alleviate a lot of the LFG pain, it doesn't always cure it. Even with 20-40 people online, it is very unlikely (unless planned prior) that you're going to have 4-6 people on the same page, in the same zone, and ready to play when you are. This is especially true on a brand new TLP server, where everyone is very spread out. A guild is a wonderful thing (guild plug time: Broken Wave) but it does not completely solve the problem of getting efficient progression on your time

I want to log in, get grouped, and get experience.

This is how you solve mitigate the problem.

  • Log in knowing where you need to be
  • When you need to be there
  • What you'll be doing and about how long you'll be doing it

Let's do this.
